FAQ

1

Would the AI read all my messages?

No. Only those that you address to @FlaiBot, and the replies to them
2

Do I need an email or phone number to sign up and invite friends?

No, just download the app and start using it. Invite friends to your group by sharing a link or by letting them scan a QR code on your phone
3

What about my privacy?

Your data is secure with us. Not using use end-to-end encryption allows us to make the app more powerful. You don’t even have a phone
4

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Not for now
5

Can I make video calls?

Not for now. It’s on our roadmap, maybe sometime in 2024
6

Is there a limit to the number of group members?

No, it’s unlimited
7

I’m not comfortable sharing my location

You can turn it off right there on the chat screen
8

How to move my chat history to another device?

You can backup your chats by linking your Apple or Google account, and then use it to continue your FlaiChat session on another device
